Men's Health Screenings at No Cost

The health screenings that could save your life are covered by your Aetna® plan.

Men face unique health risks including heart disease, prostate cancer, and colorectal cancer. Early detection through regular screenings is your best defense. Your Aetna plan provides these essential screenings at no cost to you with in-network providers.

Your Primary Care Provider: Key to Men's Health

A primary care provider (PCP) is vital for men's health, especially for prostate cancer screening and prevention. Your PCP helps detect issues early, understands your personal risk factors, and coordinates your care for optimal health outcomes.

You have multiple options to connect with a PCP:

Find an in-network provider: Visit aetna.com/find-a-doctor

Call for assistance: Contact Member Services at 877-444-1012

Virtual care option: For added convenience, you can also use Aetna Virtual Primary Care (855-835-2362)
