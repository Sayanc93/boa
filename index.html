Men's health, simplified — get the care you deserve

Your health, your future — stay ahead with preventive care

Preventive care isn't just about avoiding illness — it's about staying strong, active and ready for whatever comes next. Your Aetna® plan covers routine physical exams, prostate cancer screenings, and other preventive services at no cost to you when you visit in-network providers. These services are available at any in-network location that's convenient for you.

Why Having a Primary Care Provider (PCP) Matters

Your PCP is your healthcare partner who:

  • Knows your health history and can spot potential issues early
  • Coordinates your care with specialists when needed
  • Helps you stay on track with preventive screenings
  • Provides personalized health advice and support

Focus on Prostate Health

Prostate cancer is one of the most common cancers in men. Early detection through regular screenings can make a significant difference. Talk to your doctor about:

  • When to start prostate cancer screenings
  • Your personal risk factors
  • Available screening options

Get paid for wellness activities — up to $500

When you complete three wellness activities this year, you'll earn a wellness credit of up to $500 to apply to your medical plan premium. To earn this credit:

  • Complete an online health questionnaire

  • Get a health screening

  • Visit a primary care provider (PCP) for an annual physical

Call the My Wellness Member Service Center at 833-525-5788 or visit mywellnessresources.com to learn more and get started.

Visit a PCP without leaving home

With your Aetna Virtual Primary Care® benefit, powered by Teladoc Health, you can meet with a PCP through video at a time and place that works for you. You can also receive a no-cost blood pressure and heart rate monitor before your first visit, so you can monitor and share your vitals with your provider without leaving home.

Call 855-TELADOC (855-835-2362), visit teladochealth.com/bankofamerica or download the Teladoc Health app.
